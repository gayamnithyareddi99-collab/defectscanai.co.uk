AI-Powered Property Defect Detection & Compliance

DefectScan AI is a UK-focused SaaS platform that uses computer vision, 3D analysis, and predictive intelligence to automate property inspections, detect defects early, and ensure regulatory compliance at scale.

About Us

DefectScan AI was created to modernise property inspections using artificial intelligence and data-driven intelligence. Traditional inspection methods are time-consuming, inconsistent, and difficult to scale across large rental portfolios.

Our platform combines computer vision, predictive analytics, and compliance intelligence to deliver faster, objective, and audit-ready property insights. We help landlords, housing providers, and operators reduce risk, improve safety, and meet regulatory requirements with confidence.
