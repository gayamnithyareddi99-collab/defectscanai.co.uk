AI-Powered Property Defect Detection & Regulatory Compliance

DefectScan AI is a UK-focused SaaS platform that uses advanced computer vision, 3D analysis, and predictive intelligence to automate property inspections, detect defects early, and ensure regulatory compliance at scale.

About Us

Transforming property inspections with intelligent, data-driven technology.

DefectScan AI was built to modernise property inspections by replacing manual, inconsistent processes with accurate and scalable artificial intelligence. Traditional inspections are time-consuming, subjective, and difficult to manage across growing property portfolios.

Our platform uses computer vision, predictive analytics, and compliance intelligence to identify defects, assess risk, and generate audit-ready inspection insights in minutes. This enables landlords, housing providers, and property operators to maintain safer properties while reducing cost and operational effort.
